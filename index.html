About

Background & Identity

Ikraam Sadek is a junior lecturer at Belgium Campus IT, recognised for his adaptability and genuinely engaging approach to teaching. Growing up in the Eastern Cape, he developed a grounded perspective that informs how he connects with students from all walks of life.

Though teaching wasn't his original plan, Ikraam discovered a deep passion for education — and has never looked back. He takes pride in being the person students turn to first, offering support that extends well beyond scheduled hours.

Teaching Philosophy

Approach & Motivation

01
Discussion Over Dictation
Ikraam favours an engaging, discussion-based classroom where questions are welcomed and student voices shape the learning experience.
02
Understanding, Not Memorisation
The focus is always on conceptual depth and practical application — students are encouraged to think like developers, not recite like machines.
03
Growth Through Iteration
Feedback isn't the end — it's the beginning. Students are encouraged to fix, improve, and resubmit their work, building genuine competence over time.
04
Always Available
Ikraam makes himself accessible even beyond office hours — because great teaching doesn't always happen on a schedule.
